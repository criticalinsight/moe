Overload

Quest

By Moe
Published on October 4, 2021

It seems I lost myself Somewhere I’ve never been before, That my pieces kiss the sand Upon some unknown distant shore. I hear them softly falling For the chance to reunite, If home is where the heart is Then I have no home in sight. I know not where to find them, In which direction I shall start, Only that life appears a quest; An endless search for every part, And that the longer I stand still The more I surely slip away, So I must leave out of the fear Of what may happen if I stay —e.h.

Moe
