man, when he does not find meaning, molds satisfactory “answers” that would best fit the slip carved out from his own fingers. what we say are revelations are simply ideologies of our own species; living beings as deprived and hindered as we are. and yet, we cling to them as if they’ve been sent down from the ethereal residence of the gods, like weekly telegraphs either strengthening of changing our judgement, causing the rise or fall of a people— I speak as though I am not a sinner.
