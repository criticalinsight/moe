Longing

October 4, 2021 by Moe

there’s a chaos in longing, something lost in every patchwork of memory threaded together to stand in for what we no longer hold on to, a darkness in bringing back to life what burned nearly every fragment of hope left in our worn out bodies. A sense of vulnerability that quietly sinks in at the thought of being harmed by a mere spark of reminisce, the deathly stillness at the thought of having nothing but a memory to cling to…I can’t pinpoint the moment darkness settled, the moment we stepped into the sun and quietly withered underneath the light we held on to for far too long. There’s a world, however belittled, that exists between us, certain phases in time that can never be forgotten. but there are fragments of it that aren’t for forever, quietly fading away by the minute. And deep within that sense of freedom felt at the thought of it, there is also a sinking feeling that exists, tireless waves of unhappiness at the thought of having nothing cling to, of losing every treasured minute to the dust.

Moe
