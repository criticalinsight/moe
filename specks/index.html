Overload

Specks

By Moe
Published on October 4, 2021

it’s warm and bright and quiet and lively, just like your eyes, as green as the cypress bushes behind out backs and eyes even warmer and brighter than the rays that tickle our skin. I don’t look at you but I can almost see the specks in them, those specks I never would have known about if your mother hadn’t mentioned it two months ago. she said you claimed they were odd and you hated them. you wished you had normal eyes like everyone else’s but it’s exactly what deepened my admiration for you. you were incompatible to the others here on this planet. you were out of this world. your specks, no matter how odd, is one of the reasons why I love you this way. “have you seen anything more beautiful?” you whisper, and the leaves tremble from the sound of your voice. they wonder how a mere human being could possess a melody like that. I wonder too sometimes. a bout of the jitters, it’s what I feel whenever you speak and I don’t like it. but how could I stay away?

Moe
