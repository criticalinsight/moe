Overload

No More

By Moe
Published on October 2, 2021

I can take no more of this damnation; release me! release me from this peril! I dive headfirst into a paradise; a mere illusion. what envelopes me now is this vacuum, this emptiness. I have longed for eons the absence of life, yet now that it wallows down my blood tunnels and springs out of my fingers, I yearn for its departure. I yearn for the sun, the moon, the stars, for life and light—be it little, rare—to be pounded down with mortal faults and problems; to wail to golden rays and crystal light about the love that I have been deprived of. not this. not this blazing, fatal inferno

Moe
