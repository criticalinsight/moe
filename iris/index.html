Iris

By Moe
Published on October 4, 2021

oh infinite colorful sky, I will not degrade you of your characteristics by limiting your waves to a single blue hue, for as the sun dips into deeper cosmos, a human is warmed by the sight of your innocent red pigment. the path by which Iris dashes through, spilling an arch of magnificence signifying her presence. and then those white fluffy pieces of cotton weaved out of the hands of the powerful immortals, metamorphosing day by day— the cause of a mortal’s smile—, then the abrupt darkness: grey and darkened, a temporal, calming chaos replacing your innocence.

Moe
